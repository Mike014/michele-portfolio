EP – Somewhere I Belong

“Somewhere I Belong” is an EP born from nothing. Three tracks. No gear. No safety net. Just a computer, a demo version of Ableton Live 12, and one purchased track — Somewhere I Belong by Linkin Park.

No audio interface. No headphones. No studio. Just the need to start over. This was survival through sound. Every glitch, every rough edge, every imperfect texture was left untouched. Because this isn’t about perfection. It’s about resilience. It's about rebuilding from scratch — with nothing but noise, memory, and emotion.

Short Bio

I started making music on my own at age 20, learning by experimenting with DAWs. Later I specialized through courses such as ProducerTech and BassGorilla, focusing on modern EDM production. I have written and produced tracks for YouTubers and rappers, before moving into music and audio for films and video games. Today my work bridges electronic composition, experimental IDM/EDM, and technical sound design.
